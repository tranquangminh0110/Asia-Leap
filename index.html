Who we are

Alex Chow - Founder

Founded Asia Leap in 2006 with the vision of a group of professional experts who could be advising, facilitating and leading companies to growth and performance in Asia Pacific markets. The professionals would be at the forefront of their fields, bringing fresh insight into business opportunities and challenges that will yield positive impact. Today, Asia Leap works with a team of highly qualified professionals, assiting clients across the Asia Pacific from our headquarters Singapore.

values

01

Long-term Partnership
